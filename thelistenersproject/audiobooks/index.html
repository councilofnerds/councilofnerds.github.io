About the show

undefined

Listeners is a podcast created by the folks over at the Listeners Project and is aimed at making timeless classics of English & International literature easier to consume and appreciate on a daily/regular basis, while doing regular stuff, like, fighting for a place to stand in a packed bus or sitting idly in a slow moving train while suffering a heat stroke.
Support us and access exclusive content, only at https://councilofnerds.github.io/thelistenersproject
